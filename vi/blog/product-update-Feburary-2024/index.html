Product Updates Feburary 2024

3 tháng 2, 2024 · 1 phút để đọc

1. Editor Improvements​

Enhance your typing experience. The editor is available across our platform. Give it a try here.

1.1. A popup appears when you move to the next line​

When you move to the next line, a popup will appear on the right suggesting the next elements you should use, such as headings or images.

1.2. A popup appears when you select text​

1.3. You can add a table​

When creating a table, you can:

  • Add, remove, or edit columns
  • Add, remove, or edit rows

1.4. Add Latex​

Click to Sigma Icon

Add your LaTeX code between $$ symbols. Press "Update" to see the result below:

2. Improvements to the Exam Builder​

We have been making the attendees' view better.

To build your own exam, go to "Your Organization" > "Exams" > "Add".
